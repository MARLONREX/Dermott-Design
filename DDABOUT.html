FIELD MERGING

We are a collective of artists and planners reimagining the built environment through creative design and strategic planning. By merging these disciplines, we create spaces that are both functional and visually inspiring. Our work explores innovative urban solutions that enhance communities and improve city life. To showcase our expertise, we are developing a group portfolio featuring our best projects and ideas. This platform highlights the impact of integrated design and planning in shaping the future of urban spaces.

WHY

Merging architecture and planning is crucial to creating both beautiful and functional spaces. A clear example is Lanzarote’s Timanfaya Restaurant, an architectural landmark that struggles with poor transport infrastructure, leading to five-mile traffic queues due to inadequate parking and road access. This highlights the need for integrated design and planning to ensure that spaces are not just visually impressive but also accessible and efficient. Our approach focuses on analysis, strategic design, and collaboration to create environments that are practical, sustainable, and well-connected. By merging architecture and planning, we aim to build smarter, more efficient cities for the future.
