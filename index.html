About

Since 2020, Dermott has grown from simple graphical conceptual design into a growing practice. What began with preliminary renderings for planning applications, highlighting land use and spatial arrangement through building design, has now developed to encompass both graphic and building design for rural and urban settings. Our process guides applicants in imagining their design, creating simple visual representations of their ideas, so that they may better present their ideas to architects or engineers.
Based in the North East of England, we have expanded to projects in the South West and the Midlands. In our continued development, we explore urban matrices and emerging urbanism with people centric agonistic design to find the best use of our landscape.
