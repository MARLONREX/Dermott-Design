Conygar Quarry climbing Center . North Somerset

My first real-world project was the design of a groundbreaking climbing center featuring the tallest free-standing climbing tower in the country. It includes Olympic-scale indoor climbing and bouldering, along with over 300m² of exposed, varied rock surfaces. The building is partially underground to reduce visual impact and support local wildlife, with a rock-textured exterior that blends into the landscape.
